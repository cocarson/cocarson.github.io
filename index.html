01 / ABOUT

Fifteen years of building, from YC startups to platform teams at scale.

I'm a Senior Staff Software Engineer at Intuit Mailchimp, where I work on frontend architecture for the editors behind email, SMS, and WhatsApp. Lately I've been thinking a lot about how AI changes the way people create, and what an editor becomes when it does.

Before that: early engineering at FarmLogs (YC W12), co-founding Ballot LLC, co-creating the University of Michigan's first Product Design course, and teaching web development to refugees through RefCode.

I live in Dillon, Colorado at 9,000 feet. When I'm not writing code I'm racing mountain bikes, coaching a youth MTB team, or out with a camera chasing alpenglow.
